Optional: Harvard's Project Implicit IAT - Disability

Taking part in this activity is optional though enlightening. This test was developed by researchers at Harvard's Project Implicit international research collaborative, and it is meant to measure a person's implicit biases in their social cognition patterns. You may read the consent form on Harvard's Project Implicit test index page and decide if you would like to participate in this optional activity and contribute to Harvard's Project Implicit body of data.

If you decide you would like to participate, open and complete the linked consent form (opens in a new tab). Then, scroll down to the link that reads “IAT — Disability”.

Use the “Next” link to continue.

Privacy note: No data surrounding your decision or your test results will be passed to your library or the BTAA or any of its partner institutions. Your decision to either take or skip this optional activity will not be recorded or otherwise monitored. Your decision will not influence your progress or performance in this course.
