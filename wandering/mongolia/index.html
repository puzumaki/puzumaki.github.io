Mongolia 2005

3 June 2005 to 24 June 2005

I went to Mongolia with a group of students from the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee to study libraries and communication in Mongolia. We saw many libraries and visited television and radio stations in Ulaanbaatar, the capitol and largest city (half the population), as well as several rural areas in the Altai Mountains and Gobi Desert.
