Accessibility Crash Course

Module overview: Introduction to disability and accessibility

Welcome to Module 2: Introduction to Disability and Accessibility. In this module, you will:

  • become familiar with basic terms and concepts used to describe disability and accessibility,
  • be introduced to the idea of digital accessibility and how it can be incorporated into your daily work, and
  • recognize the changing legal landscape around accessibility as one reason why accessibility matters.

Key points

  • Accessibility is a process.
  • Accessibility is everyone's job.
  • Most importantly, accessibility impacts many people at the university.
