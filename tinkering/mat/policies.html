Ms. Aislynn Tutoring Policies

Confirmation

I send a confirmation text the day before. This is a last chance to cancel without payment. If there is no response to the text, then I assume that we are still scheduled. If you respond to the text, please do so by 11 p.m. Pacific Time.

Cancellation

Please give as much notice for cancellations as possible. I ask for 24 hours or prompt response to the confirmation text. Last minute cancellations will be charged 1/2 the original fee.

No-shows

It is the student's responsibility to show up for class on time. If a student does not show in the first 5 minutes, I will send a text. After 15 minutes, I consider it a no-show and charge 1/2 of the original fee.

Invoices

I give one month to pay an invoice. It is the paying party's responsibility to make sure the invoice is paid. If an invoice is not paid on time, tutoring is suspended until all outstanding invoices are paid. Invoices are sent at the end of the week.

Removal of students

I reserve the right to remove students from the program if the above policies are violated.

Contact

To protect student information, I do not use social media or associated sites. I can be reached by phone, text, email, and Zoom.

Pets and food

Student pets are welcome, as long as after saying hi they are not a distraction. Students are also allowed food and drinks under the same conditions. This is a fun and relaxing atmosphere to learn in.

My commitment to students and parents

I am always open to suggestions from both parents and students. In order for the tutor/student relationship to work there needs to be trust. If I cannot meet your needs I will tell you immediately. I inform parents immediately of any concerns about their student. I do not answer my phone during sessions, but I return texts and calls as soon as I am available.

Additional in-person policies

  • I cannot talk with parents before or after sessions to ensure the next session begins on time.
  • Arrive on time or early for sessions. If you are late, you will only be able to use the rest of your hour but will be charged for the whole hour.
  • Ms. Aislynn Tutoring provides one parking spot in the driveway during in-person tutoring hours. Parents may stay in the waiting area.
  • Ms. Aislynn Tutoring is ADA accessible. Let me know if any other specific accommodation(s) besides a wheelchair ramp is needed.
  • A bathroom is available for students and parents only. When the bathroom is used, hands are washed with soap and dried using the paper towels provided. Please do not use the family's personal towels. Leave the bathroom in teh same condition you found it in.
  • Privacy cannot be guaranteed for online or in-person sessions. My. Aislynn wears a headset, but students in teh waiting area may hear my side of the conversation.
  • Please cancel a session if your student is sick. Ms. Aislynn understands that students can get sick at scohol or injured and may need to cancel last minute. If you are cancelling due to illness, you will not be charged teh cancellation fee.
