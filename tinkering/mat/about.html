About Ms. Aislynn

Background

I grew up in Poway, California; near San Diego. I started my career as an educator at the age of 16, when I taught swim lessons. I soon realized that the standard programs didn't work for all students. I began modifying how I taught swimming to great success.

I went on to get a BA in Biology from Azusa Pacific University with the intention of becoming a teacher. I earned my CA Teaching Credential from California State University San Marcos then taught Middle School science.

I discovered that many students in the classroom were having the same problem as my swim students. The method of teaching did not fit their learning style. I chose to start my own tutoring business so I could meet each student where they were and help them reach their educational goals.
