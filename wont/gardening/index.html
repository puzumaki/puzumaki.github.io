Gardening

Gardening is best done with the low-maintenance no-dig edible plants, perennial where possible.

  • Fruit and nut trees. Cherry semi-dwarf, honeycrisp semi-dwarf, 5-in-1 pear tree, 5-in-1 cocktail tree, 5-in-1 plum tree, 6-in-1 apple trees.
  • Edible shrubs. Winter hardy blueberries, raspberries, blackberries, strawberry pyramid.
  • Perennial herbs. Lavender, thyme, rosemary, sage, chamomile, mint, spearmint.
  • Perrenial vegetables. Asparagus, chives, rhubarb, tomatoes (only because of squirrels and birds).
