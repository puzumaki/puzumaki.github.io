Lifestyle

Sustainable lifestyle.

  • Edible yard. Fruit trees, self-watering vegetable and herb gardens.
  • Zero waste. Recyclable packaging only, no plastic. Food scraps cooked or fed to chickens.
  • Egg-laying chickens. Eat food scraps, produce food.
  • Vegetarian sometimes vegan. Occasionally have cheese and eggs.
  • Flexible durable fashion. Children's clothing is primarily hand-me-downs and purchased from thrift or rummage sales,
