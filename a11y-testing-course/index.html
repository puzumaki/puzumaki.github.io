Accessibility Testing Course

Digital accessibility testing

You will learn about digital accessibility testing and how you can use it to complete an accessibility assessment using an easy-to-use rubric.

Finding accessibility issues is not the main goal of this process. Rather, it is important to document the issues to communicate them effectively with vendors, or to empower content owners to make the necessary changes. Most importantly, we hope that you increase your awareness of digital accessibility - what it means, why it matters, and how to incorporate it into your daily work.
