Start course >

Who should take this course?

This course is meant for graduate students, but also any university staff, faculty, or students who want a guided course to learn the basics about accessibility.

Length and pacing

The course is self-paced; you may access the content whenever is suitable for your learning. You can break the content up over a few sessions or complete it in one sitting. Remember, you can always come back to it later.

Previous participants have estimated the course may take around 2 hours to complete, depending on how much existing knowledge you may have and how much of the optional content you pursue.
