Skill 1 practice

Images can add context, clarity, and meaning to a document; in other words, when used correctly, images enhance the accessibility of your document.

Practice 1: simple graphic

Here is a photograph and three different contexts it might be found in. Consider what you would highlight in each scenario. What alternative text would you write and what, if anything, would you add as adjacent text?

  1. On a tourism site.
  2. In a geology lecture.
  3. For a personal photography site.

Practice hints

Information that could be useful for each of the scenarios:

  • For a tourism site: This is a photo of Jay Cooke State Park in northern Minnesota.
  • For a geology lecture: The prominent rock type in the photo is slate and metagraywacke bedrock from the Fond Du Lac and Thomson formations.
  • For a person photography site: The camera used to take the photo was a Nikon D1X with a 14mm f/2.8 lens at a 160 ISO.

These details are why it's important for the author of the content to be as involved as possible. They will have the expertise and know what information is most useful, otherwise the best another person might manage is something like "Water swiftly running over large rocks in a misty forest."

Practice 2: complex graphic

Try to imagine what this complex graphic may look like after reading the alternative text and its adjacent paragraph.

Alternative text: "Scatter plot chart of Stephen King’s titles with book ratings versus movie ratings."

Adjacent paragraph describing the trends: “Many of Stephen King’s novels have become movies. Based on the average ratings people have given each title on IMDb, a movie rating website, and GoodReads, which has book ratings, most books and movies align on whether it was good or bad. Dreamcatcher, Thinner, Cujo, and The Dark Half all performed poorly both as movies and as books, where The Shining, Stand By Me, The Green Mile, and Shawshank Redemption all performed well as both books and movies. Dolores Claiborne performed slightly better as a movie than a book, and the Stand performed far better as a book. Shawshank Redemption performed the best overall.”

Practice answer

Did the paragraph capture the trend you see in the image? When there’s this much data, also include access to a table of the raw data.
