Skill 2: practice

Make sure foreground and background colors and other visual indicators contrast each other.

For those with vision and light sensitivities, the text with poor contrast is presented below in exercise number one as an example of inaccessible content.

Practice 1: Identify low contrast

Which of the following has bad contrast against the default background color of this page (white color, #ffffff)? Hint: you can use WebAIM's color contrast checker to help.

  1. Stephen King has written over 65 books (yellow color #f5f01f, 12 point non bold),
  2. and at least 50 movie adaptations made from King's works (red color #ff3d3d, 14 point bold).
  3. About 30 TV series or miniseries have also been made so far (color #a11010, 18 point non bold).
Text color contrast answer

Both a and b. In order for text to meet minimum contrast, the ratio must be 4.5:1. A has a contrast ratio of 1.2:1. B has a 3.5:1 contrast ratio. C has a good contrast ratio of 8.08:1, which also passes the WCAG AAA level minimum of 7:1.

Practice 2: Calculate the contrast ratio

Calculate the contrast ratio for the following two colors: #ae26c0 and #ffe8b8. Hint: you can use WebAIM's color contrast checker to help.

Color contrast ratio answer

With the foreground color #ae26c0 and background color #ffe8b8, the contrast ratio is 4.55:1. This passes WCAG 2.1 AA.
