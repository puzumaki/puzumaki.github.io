Skill 4: practice

Sighted users visually scan a page of text in order to find helpful links. Make sure that people who use assistive technology like screen readers can have an equivalent experience by embedding concise, descriptive links

Practice

Identify correct and incorrect examples of linking in the following paragraph:

The Big Ten Academic Alliance (BTAA) (https://btaa.org/) currently includes eighteen universities in the midwestern and eastern United States. The BTAA was established by the presidents of the Big Ten Conference in 1958. It includes such well known universities as Northwestern University; Penn State University; and the University of Minnesota. In addition, you can click here, here, and here for links to the websites for the Universities of Maryland, Michigan, and Wisconsin respectively. The Big Ten Conference is growing and so will, probably, the membership of the BTAA. For example, the University of Southern California may be becoming a member of the BTAA soon. Finally, you can contact the BTAA for more information at info@btaa.org.

Good links answer
  • Correct (5 links): BTAA, Northwestern University, Penn State University, University of Minnesota, info@btaa.org
  • Incorrect (5 links): https://btaa.org, here, here, here, "For example, the University of Southern California may be becoming a member of the BTAA soon"
