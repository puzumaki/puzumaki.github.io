Skill 3: practice

Heading levels function like levels in an outline. When the content of a page is complex, a logical heading structure can make a big difference in how well readers understand its meaning and the relationship between its parts.

Practice 1

Below is a nested list that represents the outline for an essay titled "Symbols of Freedom in Shawshank Redemption". Assign different heading levels, anywhere between heading level 1 and heading level 6, to the outline.

  • Symbols of Freedom in Shawshank Redemption
    • Introduction
      • Theme of freedom
      • Contrast freedom with unfair imprisoning
    • Bird as the struggle of human spirit
      • Bird as a universal symbol of freedom
      • “Caging” the bird as a metaphor
    • Library as a symbol for community
